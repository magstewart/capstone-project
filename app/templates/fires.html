Wildfires in Washington State

Over 1,000 wildfires occur in Washington every year, costing tax payers more than $60 million.

State investigators are tasked with determining the cause of each wildfire, with the goal of recovering the containment cost for fires that were started due to human activity.

The goal of this project is to find a way to reduce the workload and cost of investigations while still recovering the funds from those fires that were caused by humans.
